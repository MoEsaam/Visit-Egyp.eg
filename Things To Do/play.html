Parks

Open parks are one of the most important places for recreational tourism, as they introduce you to the people you go to and help you adapt quickly

Amusement Park

The amusement park is one of the best places of recreational tourism that the tourist enjoys by hiking, playing and taking souvenir photos

Hotels and Resorts

Hotels and resorts are among the most important places of recreational tourism, as they are the basis of any tourism, especially entertainment

Shopping

Shopping is one of the most important tourist destinations, especially entertainment tourism, as it is the backbone of tourism in general

Cairo Tower

The Cairo Tower is located on the island of Zamalek in Giza. It consists of 16 floors and is 187 meters high. Tourists can enjoy the panoramic views that the tower offers to the city of a thousand minarets. There are also telescopes

Mall of Egypt

Mall of Egypt is the most important entertainment destination in the Middle East It is located in the 6th of October City. It covers an area of 165,000 square metres, can accommodate 6,500 cars, and owns the first indoor ski slope in Africa

Giza Zoo

Giza Zoo is the largest and first zoo in Africa and was built on 180 acres. The park includes many types of animals, including wild and amphibious such as tigers, crocodiles, birds, elephants and others ,and founded in 1891 During the reign of Khedive Tawfiq

Dream Park

Dream Park is located in the Sixth of October City. The Dream Park theme park accommodates 30,000 visitors per day and includes a wide range of different entertainment games for all ages, even your child can enjoy through a full park for children only.

ElMoez Street

Al-Moez Street is considered one of the most important historical streets in Cairo, because it contains important Islamic archaeological sites between the Fatimid and Mamluk eras and It is also considered the largest Islamic open museum in the world.

North Coast

The North Coast is considered one of the most important coasts of Africa and extends for an area of 1050 km in the north of Egypt and overlooks the Mediterranean Sea with its clear blue waters and there are many villages, resorts and tourist hotels

Intrenational Garden

The International Garden is one of the most famous gardens and parks in Cairo and is considered one of the most beautiful tourist places in Cairo. The garden includes many sections containing trees, animals and plants that are famous for many countries around the world.

Dahab

The city of Dahab is located in the south of Sinai on the Gulf of Aqaba and was given this name because of the golden color of its sand And there is the nature reserve Ras Abu Galum and the Blue-hall area, which is the most famous diving site in the world
