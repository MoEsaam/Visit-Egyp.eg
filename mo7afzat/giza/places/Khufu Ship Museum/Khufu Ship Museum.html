DISCOVER THE BEST OF "Khufu Ship Museum"



Khufu Boat Museum or Giza Solar Boat Museum was built in 1985 AD. It is a museum dedicated to displaying the reconstruction of Khufu's solar ship.

Contents:

Reassembled Khufu ship (the ship was fully assembled in 1968) (the ship was disassembled around the funeral rites into 1,224 small pieces before being buried near the pyramids in the boat pit). The museum is built in order to enable the boat to be viewed from three different levels on three floors. On the ground floor, one can look at the bottom of the boat. Architectural model of the Khufu solar ship. Discovery pictures and reassembly of the ship.
