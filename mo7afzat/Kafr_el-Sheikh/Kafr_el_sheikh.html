Burullus Lighthouse

The Burullus lighthouse is one of the oldest lighthouses in Egypt, as this lighthouse is the only surviving group of lighthouses built by Khedive Abbas during the nineteenth century.

Its establishment dates back to 1869 AD and is 120 years old. Its height is 55 meters and is installed on 3 bases of lead in the form of a triangle, weighing 40 tons.

In addition to a set of iron springs to help it with flexibility and inclination for a distance of 12 cm in strong winds.
