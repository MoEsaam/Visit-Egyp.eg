The 5 Best Cafés In Alexandria



Portofino Restaurant & Cafe

Portofino Cafe is also one of the best cafes in Alexandria to try. It has two branches, the first on Army Street, Sidi Bishr, and the second in the Camp Chezar area. The cafe is unique and rated 4.4 stars by customers who try it.

The place offers a varied menu with many drinks and juices, in addition to meals for breakfast or lunch, as well as sushi. As well as a special selection of sweets. The café is nice and tidy, and the ambiance is beautiful and distinctive. Its prices are relatively high for the service it provides. It is a suitable place for families and individuals.

Portofino Alexandria Café timings on all days of the week, 24 hours a day.

The branch in the northeast is for western food and the other is for Italian food




Delices

Delice Cafe is one of the most famous Alexandria cafes at all. It is an old and elegant café with a unique and distinctive character among the city's cafés. The place is also very popular with foreign tourists and visitors to Alexandria from all over. It is a symbol of Alexandria, and an important tourist destination. The reason for this is due to its distinguished location in the Raml Station area in Alexandria, and its elegant Greek-style design and decorations.

The cafe is quiet and the music is quiet and unobtrusive. It is characterized by the presence of high-end indoor sessions suitable for families and groups, and other outdoor sessions overlooking the Raml Station area and the Alexandria Corniche, where the beautiful atmosphere is outdoors. The cafe offers a wonderful menu of hot and cold drinks, in addition to baked goods, pastries, and various delicious desserts, in addition to meals for breakfast and lunch.

The service in the cafe is very upscale, and the staff is respectful and friendly towards customers. You can also buy what you want to take away. The prices of the place are relatively high but very suitable for the excellent service provided, as well as the magnificence of the café seating and the beautiful ambiance that is unparalleled in Alexandria. Which makes it one of the unique cafes, which are frequented by many customers, whether morning or evening. Working hours of Delice Café in Alexandria are seven days a week, from 8 in the morning until 12 midnight. The place is wonderful and worth trying and tasting its irresistible desserts.




Starbucks

Starbucks Café San Stefano branch, one of Alexandria's finest cafés. It is located in a privileged location and one of the most luxurious and luxurious places in Alexandria. The view of the place is amazing, and the ambiance is the most beautiful ever. The café is distinguished by its elegant modern decor, and neat and tidy furniture. The prices are relatively high for the service and quality that the café provides.

The café is suitable for young people or families, and its sessions are distinguished and arranged. Starbucks offers a special menu, which includes a variety of drinks, ice cream and desserts. If you intend to visit Alexandria soon, we advise you to try the place and enjoy the atmosphere surrounding the place overlooking the Alexandria Corniche.

Starbucks Cafe - San Stefano Alexandria working hours, seven days a week, 24 hours a day.




View Cafe

View Cafe is one of the very popular Alexandria cafes. The view of the café is nice and the service is good, but the prices are relatively high. It is worth noting that the place has a “minimum charge” for one person, which is 50 Egyptian pounds. The cafe's menu is distinctive and varied, and provides many drinks and food, as well as some types of delicious desserts. The view of the place is wonderful at sunset, which attracts a lot of customers.

The café is located inside the famous Mediterranean Azur Hotel in Alexandria, El Guish Road. The menu includes many drinks and delicious foods such as hot cedar, hot chocolate, Turkish coffee, mocha, brownies and many more.

View Cafe in Alexandria working hours on all days of the week from 8 am to 3 am. Except on Sundays, Mondays and Tuesdays, it starts at 7:30 in the morning until 3:00 in the morning.




Brazilian Coffee

The Brazilian Coffee Café is one of the famous and old Alexandria cafes, as the place dates back to 1929 AD. It is the first shop to sell ground coffee in Alexandria. The place has two floors, and the ambiance is lovely. Alexandria Corniche is 1.9 km away. It has more than one branch in the city. The café is ideal for coffee lovers of all kinds, as the café uses the finest coffee.

The décor of the indoor venue is elegant, with style and ambiance dating back to the 1970s. The seating is indoor only, and sometimes there is a special guitarist in the room, which gives the place a wonderful dreamy ambiance. The prices are reasonable and affordable. And the service is very good. The place is distinguished by its location in the middle of the old and beautiful Alexandria buildings. It offers a special menu, where a lot of delicious drinks, especially coffee drinks. However, one of the biggest drawbacks of the place is the crowding.

Brazilian coffee café in Alexandria working hours on all days of the week from 7 in the morning until 11 in the evening.
