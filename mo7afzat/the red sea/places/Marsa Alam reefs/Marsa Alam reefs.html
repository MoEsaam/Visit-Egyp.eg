DISCOVER THE BEST OF "Marsa Alam reefs"



“Marsa Alam” quickly became the word of choice among the international divers community, because here there are fewer divers, healthier coral reefs, large number of near-surface fish, unique coral towers, exciting divers landing points, attractive caves and reef-covered shipwrecks. Coral.. All this made Marsa Alam the most wonderful vacation destination in the Red Sea. The area here is wide and varied, as it is known that Marsa Alam is an extension of the coast with a length of about 100 km. Well-established resorts, campgrounds, and dive centers are spread north and south of the main village, some of which are located a considerable distance away. All levels of diving courses are available in Marsa Alam. Marsa Alam is also the starting point in Egypt for yacht safari trips to remote areas at sea and the southern regions. The most attractive areas are the three islands: Daedalus, Rocky, and Aquamarine; In addition to the two coral reefs: Staih and Saint John. The Marsa Alam marine area is protected, like many other areas in Egypt, and the diving centers here operate according to environmentally friendly procedures. Since 1995, local authorities, NGOs and international entities such as the US Agency for International Development have collaborated to conserve marine life. Currently, there are regular checks and updates to ensure the presence of a buoy at all dive sites.
