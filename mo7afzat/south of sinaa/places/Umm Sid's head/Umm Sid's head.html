For the beach itself is called "Golden Beach". This is how it is indicated on many maps, but among tourists, for ease of perception and indication of the exact place, the name "Ras Umm al-Sid" comes in the name of the bay. The name exactly describes the type and color of sand. The entire beach consists of sand of bright golden hues. Sand grains are medium in size, in some places they may resemble pebbles. Such is the case for the edges of the beach to the west and east, where it is surrounded by low rocky cliffs, sandstone reefs, and coral deposits. It is one of the best beaches of Sharm El Sheikh and one of the most important tourist destinations in it, as it is the location you want to find yourself in if you are planning a real relaxing vacation in Sharm El Sheikh away from the crowds It is located on the tip of a small peninsula, 5 km from Naama Bay It contains amazing landscapes of the Red Sea and coral reefs Divers go to see the amazing coral reefs, as well as it contains a different group of rare fish
