DISCOVER THE BEST OF "King Tut Museum"



The girl’s tomb is one of the attractive tourist areas in South Sinai Governorate. The name has an exciting story that talks about a love story between a girl from the city of Dahab and a foreign tourist... The legend says that the hero of the story is a girl from the city of Dahab who fell in love with a foreign young man at a time when customs prohibited such things. One of the customs of her tribe is that the girl does not go out of the house after the age of 12, and they have a famous saying that says, “Our girls are like jewels, they do not appear.” After the girl’s family learned about this story, they exaggerated her punishment until the girl escaped and dived into the depths of the sea. One of the accounts mentioned that the coral reefs closed on her as a punishment for her sin, and another version said that the girl tied herself under the water to escape the punishment of her family, so this was called The area is called the "Girl's Tomb". The Qabr Al-Bint area is located in the south of Dahab, 20 kilometers from the city, near the Three Bowls and Wadi Jana. The "Tomb of the Girl" area is considered one of the best places for diving in Egypt, and it is one of the tourist destinations that tourists prefer to visit on one-day trips during their tours in Sharm El-Sheikh and Dahab.
