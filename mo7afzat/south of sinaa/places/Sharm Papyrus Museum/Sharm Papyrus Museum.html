DISCOVER THE BEST OF "Sharm Papyrus Museum"



Sharm Papyrus Museum is a unique museum in Sharm El Sheikh, which hosts a large collection of papyrus and sells copies to visitors as souvenirs. And invitations and messages dating back to the Pharaonic times, which explain the customs and traditions of the pharaohs, along with pictures of kings and queens, and the museum also reviews the interesting history of papyrus making.
The Sharm Papyrus Museum draws the attention of tourists from outside Egypt in particular. During their tour of the antique market. The museum gives visitors the opportunity to learn about the social life of the ancient Egyptians through dozens of pictures and drawings engraved on papyrus, which are perfectly replicated.
