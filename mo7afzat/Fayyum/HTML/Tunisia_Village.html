Tunisia Village El-Fayyom



Do you want to travel to Tunisia? You can do that without leaving Egypt. In Egypt, there is a distinctive village called Tunis Village, which is a verse in beauty, and its name was derived from green Tunisia. So, with a small budget, you can spend several days or a day trip in Tunis, Fayoum, and enjoy the beauty and distinctive activities.

Who is Tunisia Village?
It is a village only 50 km from Fayoum and it follows it. The village is small and has a limited number of houses, but all of them are of one style of construction and coordination so that you feel that you are in an organized foreign village with oriental features.


Evelyn Bure is a Swiss woman who came to work as a farmer
When you go there and ask about Evelyn Bure, you will find that everyone knows her. She is the Swiss lady who came to the village in the sixties and found her a poor and simple village, but she fell in love with her. She was the wife of the famous poet Sayed Hijab at the time, but she decided to live in the village even after her divorce from him, she and her two children had long lived From her ex-foreign husband in Fayoum.

She began planning to unite the mud houses into creative ceramic shapes, and established a school to teach the art of ceramics to children and adults, and so, day by day, the village turned into a distinctive tourist destination.

At the same time, Evelyn turned into a real Egyptian peasant who cultivates the land, deals with farm animals, speaks Egyptian dialect, lives in a simple country house of mud and still makes pottery to this day.


What do I do in Tunisia village ?
You may wonder about the activities that you can do in Tunis village to enjoy your time there, so I will list for you a number of simple and distinctive pleasures that you can spend a simple and nice time there, and the biggest advantage is that all of this can be implemented with a limited budget.

There are large green spaces. The village was named after Tunisia due to its distinctive vegetation and greenery, which made it compared to the city of Tunis. There you will find coordinated green spaces and gardens for roaming and picnicking among the trees, especially olive trees. You can spend a day for a picnic, playing and recreation.

You can spend the day in the most beautiful place of Lake Qarun, on which the village of Tunis is located. You can also go to the waterfalls and Wadi El Rayan and have a good time. If you do not like to swim, you can rent a boat in the lake to go on a very beautiful tour there.

The village of Tunis is famous for its distinctive ceramics industry, hundreds of shapes and colors of antiques and utensils, it is difficult to go there without passing through the ceramics school to play a little with clay and form what you want from it and see it burning to bring out a masterpiece for you, as well as buying professional souvenirs from pots and ceramic antiques.

In the village of Tunis, there are many places that offer fun safari offers such as camping, barbecue, Bedouin kicking, Tanoura and other shows, as well as you can rent horses to enjoy their launch through the green spaces of the village.

There are wide options there. You can rent a house, a small villa or a chalet on the lake. You can also live in hotels of the highest level, some of which are distinguished by luxury and the other emphasizes the distinctive oriental character to complete the creative picture of the trip.

You can book hotels or chalets online for your special trip, and there are dozens of offers for one-day trips to the village if you want to go without overnighting.
