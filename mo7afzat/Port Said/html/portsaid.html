The Military Museum Of Port Said

The foundation of Port-Said Military Museum dates back to 1964, and commemorates the tripartite aggression on Port-Said city in 1956. The museum was inaugurated on the national day of Port-Said governorate on the 23rd of December, 1964. The museum went through renovations, maintenance and development and was reopened in 1978
