Salt Mountain



The Salt Mountain attracts visitors and tourists from all over the country, who come to spend the most beautiful and enjoyable times on ski adventures and take the most beautiful souvenir photos. Jabal al-Malah is one of the most important tourist sites that contributed to the revival of the city's economy, which resulted from the saltah area, which was established in 1859 AD. In winter, you can visit the mountain and benefit from sitting on the salt, which pulls negative energy from the body, and these natural treatment sessions are similar to salt caves to treat diseases.
